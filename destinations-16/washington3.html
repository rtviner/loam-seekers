Bellingham

On day 8 we continued our journey north and left Seattle for Bellingham WA. It would have been easy to drive 3 hrs to Vancouver BC that morning but we heard that Bellingham was worth checking out. Upon arriving we stopped into a local bike shop, Kulshan Cycles, to buy a local trail map. The guys at the bike shop offered us a tons of advice on trails to check out and recommended an awesome camping spot that we would have never found otherwise.
