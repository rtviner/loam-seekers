Issaquah

We camped again in Liberty and left for Issaquah (east of Seattle) in the morning of day 6. Paul's friend Kris drove up from Oregon and met us in the Tiger Mountain parking lot around noon. From the lot, we climbed our bikes up Master Link, a really nice gradual uphill only trail, to Predator, a gnarly Downhill Only trail.

Seattle

After riding Tiger Mountain we headed to Seattle. Aunt Sharon was gracious enough to house us and our bikes, show us around the city, and feed us too. Some of the highlights in Seattle were showers for the first time in days. Fresh ginger beer on tap, real sourdough bread, local bratwurst, and prosciutto. Mmmmmm.
